News and Notes

Parking Permits

Please email summerfieldhoa@ymail.com for your parking permit(s). The HOA Board of Directors will be walking the property to assure vehicles have valid parking permits. Vehicles without permits are subject to be towed.

Dumpsters

We are aware that each month this is an issue, however, there continues to be violators leaving items outside the dumpster and filling the dumpster and the lid not closing. Again, there will be no warning, an automatic $100 fine for overfilling the dumpster and $500 for leaving items outside the dumpster. NO EXCEPTIONS! Homeowners, please inform your tenants.

Laundry Appliances

The HOA will be replacing the washers and dryers in the units in the next few weeks. The appliances will no longer be free, there will be a charge for the washer and dryer. Details are still under review, just letting homeowners know, new appliances will be arriving.
